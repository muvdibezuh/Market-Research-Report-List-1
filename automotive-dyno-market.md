<p><strong>The Automotive Dyno Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engine Dynamometer</li><li>Chassis Dynamometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dynamometer market encompasses two main types: engine dynamometers and chassis dynamometers. Engine dynamometers test the performance of an engine independently from the vehicle, measuring parameters like horsepower and torque in a controlled environment. In contrast, chassis dynamometers assess the entire vehicle's performance, simulating real-world driving conditions to evaluate power output at the wheels, fuel efficiency, and emissions. Both types are essential for research, development, and tuning within the automotive industry, addressing specific performance metrics and compliance testing.</p></p>
