<p><p>The Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLD) market is primarily segmented into Surface Mount Technology (SMT/SMD) CPLDs and Through Hole CPLDs. SMT/SMD CPLDs are designed for modern electronic applications that require compact, high-density solutions, making them ideal for automation and consumer electronics. In contrast, Through Hole CPLDs are utilized in applications that demand robust connections and ease of manual soldering, often found in industrial and legacy systems. Both types cater to different design requirements and production processes in the electronics industry.</p></p>
