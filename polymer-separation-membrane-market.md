<p><p>The polymer separation membrane market comprises three main types: microfiltration (MF), ultrafiltration (UF), and reverse osmosis (RO). Microfiltration targets larger particles, such as bacteria and suspended solids, effectively removing them from liquids. Ultrafiltration offers finer separation, capturing smaller molecules, including viruses and colloids. Reverse osmosis utilizes pressure to eliminate dissolved salts and organic molecules, achieving high purity levels in water treatment. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like water purification, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
