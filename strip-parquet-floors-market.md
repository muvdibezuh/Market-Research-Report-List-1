<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Strip Parquet Floors market is primarily categorized into two types: engineered and solid. Engineered strip parquet consists of multiple layers, with a hardwood veneer on top, providing enhanced stability and resistance to moisture. This makes it suitable for various environments. Solid strip parquet, on the other hand, is made from a single piece of hardwood, offering authenticity and longevity but is more vulnerable to humidity changes. Both types cater to different preferences and applications in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
