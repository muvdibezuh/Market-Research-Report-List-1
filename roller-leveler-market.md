<p><p>The roller leveler market is categorized into two main types: two-roll and multi-roll systems. Two-roll levelers use two rolls to flatten materials, offering a simpler design and lower initial cost, making them suitable for lighter applications. In contrast, multi-roll levelers utilize several rolls to provide more uniform pressure and improved flattening capabilities, ideal for thicker, harder materials. This differentiation allows manufacturers to choose a system that best meets their production needs and material specifications.</p></p>
