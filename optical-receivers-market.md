<p><strong>The Optical Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical receivers market can be categorized into three main types: Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (PDH), Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH), and Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (SPDH). PDH systems manage data transmission with slightly varying clock rates, allowing for flexibility in speed. SDH offers a more synchronized approach, enabling efficient data transport across long distances. SPDH combines elements of both, facilitating seamless connectivity in networks that require synchronization while accommodating diverse data rates. Each type caters to specific network requirements and infrastructure needs.</p></p>
