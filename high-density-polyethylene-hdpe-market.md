<p><p>High-density polyethylene (HDPE) is a versatile thermoplastic used in various applications across different molding processes. In blow molding, HDPE is shaped into hollow items like bottles by inflating heated plastic within a mold. Injection molding involves forcing melted HDPE into molds to create solid, complex parts, commonly used in containers and industrial components. Extrusion molding continuously shapes HDPE into profiles, pipes, or sheets, making it ideal for construction and packaging industries. Together, these methods cater to diverse market needs for HDPE products.</p></p>
