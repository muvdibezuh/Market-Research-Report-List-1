<p><p>LED curing lights are specialized dental instruments used to polymerize light-sensitive materials, such as dental resins and adhesives, quickly and efficiently. These lights emit high-intensity blue light, which activates photoinitiators in composite materials, ensuring rapid hardening and adhesion during dental procedures. Their advantages include shorter curing times, reduced heat generation, and greater energy efficiency compared to traditional halogen lights.</p><p>The LED Curing Light Market is on a growth trajectory, with a projected CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period.
