<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Water Meter</li><li>Electromagnetic Water Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless water meter market features primarily two types: ultrasonic water meters and electromagnetic water meters. Ultrasonic meters use sound waves to measure flow rate, offering high accuracy and low maintenance. They are ideal for residential and commercial applications. Electromagnetic meters, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic fields to measure the velocity of conductive fluids, making them suitable for industrial uses. Both types enhance efficiency in water management, providing real-time data for better resource allocation and monitoring.</p></p>
