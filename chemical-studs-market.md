<p><p>The **Chemical Studs Market** includes **Injectable Adhesive Anchors** and **Capsule Adhesive Anchors**, both used for secure structural fastening. Injectable anchors involve injecting adhesive into drilled holes, offering flexibility and strong bonding for heavy loads. Capsule anchors use pre-measured adhesive capsules, activated by insertion of studs, ensuring precise application and consistent performance. Both types are widely used in construction, infrastructure, and industrial applications, providing reliable, durable, and vibration-resistant solutions for anchoring in concrete, masonry, and other substrates.</p></p>
