<p><strong>The Compost Turners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum Compost Turners</li><li>Elevating Face Compost Turners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compost turners market includes various types designed to enhance composting efficiency. Drum compost turners utilize a rotating drum to mix and aerate organic materials, ensuring uniform decomposition. Elevating face compost turners, on the other hand, use a raised turning mechanism that lifts and aerates the compost pile, promoting better airflow and heat distribution. Both types cater to different scales of composting operations, from small-scale facilities to large commercial composting sites, improving material breakdown and reducing processing time.</p></p>
