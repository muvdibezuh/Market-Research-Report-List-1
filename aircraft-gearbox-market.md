<p><p>The aircraft gearbox market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. The Reduction Gearbox (RGB) reduces engine speed for propeller or rotor efficiency. The Accessory Gearbox (AGB) drives essential components like generators and pumps. Actuation Gearboxes facilitate control surface movements, enhancing aircraft maneuverability. Tail Rotor Gearboxes are vital in helicopters, transferring power to the tail rotor for stability and control. Other gearbox types include specialized designs for unique applications, ensuring optimized performance and reliability across different aircraft platforms.</p></p>
