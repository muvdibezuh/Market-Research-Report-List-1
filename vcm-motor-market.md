<p><p>The VCM (Voice Coil Motor) market is categorized into two main types: AF (Autofocus) and OIS (Optical Image Stabilization). AF VCMs are primarily used in cameras and smartphones to adjust the focus quickly and accurately, enhancing image clarity. OIS VCMs, on the other hand, stabilize images by counteracting camera shake during capturing, ensuring smoother visuals in photos and videos. These technologies are essential for improving camera performance in various consumer electronics, driving advancements in imaging capabilities.</p></p>
