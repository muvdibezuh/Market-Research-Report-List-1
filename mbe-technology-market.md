<p><ul><li>Normal MBE Systems</li><li>Laser MBE Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MBE (Molecular Beam Epitaxy) technology encompasses two primary market types: Normal MBE Systems and Laser MBE Systems. Normal MBE Systems utilize thermal sources to deposit thin films of materials on substrates, allowing for precise control over layer thickness and composition. In contrast, Laser MBE Systems integrate laser technology to enhance material deposition rates and improve film quality. This differentiation enables specialized applications in semiconductor manufacturing, optoelectronics, and nanotechnology, catering to varying industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
