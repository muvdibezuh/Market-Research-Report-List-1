<p><p>Classic motor yachts can be categorized into three primary market types based on their hull designs: </p><p>1. **Displacement Hull**: These yachts travel at slower speeds, gliding through the water and providing a stable, comfortable ride. </p><p>2. **Planing Hull**: Designed for higher speeds, these hulls lift out of the water, allowing for swift travel and agility. </p><p>3. **Semi-Displacement Hull**: Combining features of both, they can cruise efficiently at moderate speeds and can plane under certain conditions, offering versatility in performance and comfort.</p></p>
