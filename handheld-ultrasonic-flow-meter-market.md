<p><ul><li>Clamp</li><li>Clip-on</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld ultrasonic flow meters are classified primarily into clamp-on and clip-on types. Clamp-on flow meters are designed to attach externally to the pipe surface, allowing for non-intrusive measurements without process interruption. They are versatile and suitable for various pipe materials and sizes. In contrast, clip-on models are more compact and often intended for smaller pipes, offering portability and ease of use in field applications. Both types provide accurate flow measurements, ideal for maintenance and troubleshooting in fluid systems.</p></p>
