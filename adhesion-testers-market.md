<p><p>The adhesion testers market encompasses various types of equipment designed to evaluate the adhesive properties of materials. Tensile testing machines measure the force required to pull two surfaces apart, while friction testing machines assess the resistance between surfaces under load. Peel testing machines examine the adhesive strength at the interface of two bonded materials. Multifunctional testing machines combine multiple testing capabilities into one device, allowing for comprehensive assessments. Other specialized testers address specific adhesive challenges, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
